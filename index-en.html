Process.

Our expertise lies in handcrafted rugs made from durable New Zealand wool, which can be customized in design, color, size, shape, and texture to perfectly match your vision and ideas. Uniquely crafted. Durable in use.

01 — Idea

Brief & Sketch

A sketch, a mood, an idea. We take any form of input and develop it into a concrete design concept for your custom rug.

02 — Craft

Handmade Production

Each rug is meticulously handcrafted by experienced artisans who blend traditional manufacturing techniques with contemporary aesthetics.

Part of Your Story

The result: a product that not only complements your interior but becomes part of its story. Built to be lived with, built to last.

About

thegrey
.berlin

We're from Berlin – and we think like it. Edgy. Restless. Free-spirited. Never pinned down to a single direction, and never afraid to start a project without a map.

Every rug begins wide open. No briefs we won't entertain, no shapes we won't try, no colours we won't put next to one another. We move in every direction at once – exploring, sketching, questioning – until something starts to take form.

Then, together with you – whether you're a private client, a designer, an architect, or a partner studio – we pull the threads tight. We sharpen the idea. We distil the noise into one piece, one concept, one statement.

Open at the beginning. Conceptual at the end. Free of restrictions all the way through.

Berlin taught us that the best things rarely come from following the rules. So we don't.
